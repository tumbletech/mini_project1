About Us

Share A Page is an online bookstore founded by a group of Filipino professionals whose advocacy is to spread accurate knowledge and information to as many people as it can. The aim is to combat the spread of disinformation known nowadays as Fake News.

This is done by exploiting the most powerful tool in the world today - the internet.

To achieve this goal, books at this site is sold at significantly lower cost than their current market price.

Not only are the site visitors encouraged to buy but also sell their pre-owned books. (subject to the site's terms and conditions)

Tired of keeping your old books? Talk to us by clicking the Trade button, tell us something about your old book and we will get back to you.
