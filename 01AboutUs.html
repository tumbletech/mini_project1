About Us

We are a group of professionals whose advocacy is increasing literacy among Filipinos. We do this by making peer-reviewed, high level books and other reading materials available to as many Filipinos as we can.

We do this by exploiting the most powerful tool in the world now - the internet.

Not only are we buying books to sell, but we are also trading books - for books too. Interested? Please click the trade button to show us the cover of the book you want to trade and give a short description about and we will get back to you.
